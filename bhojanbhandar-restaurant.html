Our Story

12 Saal Ki Kahani

From a small 4-table dhaba to a full family restaurant — our journey started with one clay tandoor and a dream.

2010
Year We
Started
Our Journey

Ek Sapna, Ek Tandoor, Bahut Pyaar

In 2010, Ramesh bhaiya started Bhojan Bhandar with 4 tables, 1 gas stove, and a recipe book inherited from his mother. The dream was simple — serve the best ghar ka khana to people who miss home-cooked food.

Today we serve 300+ customers daily. The recipes haven't changed. The masala is still hand-ground every morning. The ghee is still pure desi. Only the number of tables has grown — from 4 to 40.

✦

"Agar khana dil se banao, toh khaane wale zaroor wapas aate hain."

— Ramesh Yadav, Founder

Our Promise

Hamaari Values

These are not just words on a wall. These are the things we check every single day before opening the shutter.

🌿
Fresh Ingredients, Daily
Vegetables from the sabzi mandi every morning. No frozen stock, ever. If we run out, we run out — we don't compromise on freshness.
💰
Honest Pricing
Full meal under ₹150. Thali at ₹299. We believe good food shouldn't be only for rich people — it should be for everyone.
🫕
Recipes From Heart
Every recipe is from our family's kitchen. No shortcuts, no packet masala, no MSG. Hand-ground spices, slow cooking, the traditional way.
😊
Customer is Family
If your food is not right, we remake it. Period. Our regular customers are like family — we remember their names, preferences, and even their birthdays.
🌍
Zero Wastage Policy
Leftover food is donated to nearby construction workers every evening. We cook with purpose and serve with responsibility.
📱
Easy to Order
WhatsApp pe message karo, paisa UPI se do, ghar pe lo. Koi app nahi, koi jhanjhat nahi. Seedha aur saral.
